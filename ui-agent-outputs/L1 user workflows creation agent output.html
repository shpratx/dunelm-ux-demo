Scenario 1 Loyalty Programme Enrolment

Scenario 1

Loyalty Programme Enrolment

A long-tenure Next customer who has never held a loyalty account discovers the programme and decides to sign up. She wants to join without being required to open a nextpay credit account, and needs to see a clear statement about how her data — including returns history — will be used before she commits.

User Goal

Join the Next loyalty programme quickly and confidently, knowing her data will not be used against her, and start earning points immediately

Business Goal

Convert long-tenure cash and debit customers into loyalty members and establish their digital identity within the programme

Workflow: Variation 1 — Self-Service Online Enrolment Customer discovers and joins via next.co.uk independently

Workflow Steps

1.0 Loyalty Landing Page (Join Now) 2.0 Programme Overview (Continue) 3.0 Data Use Statement (I Understand — Continue) 4.0 Enrolment Form (Submit) {if email already registered} Er.1 Account Exists (Sign In / Merge) 5.0 Confirmation & QR Code (Go to Dashboard) 6.0 Loyalty Dashboard (Start Earning)

Programme Overview

Goal

  • Customer evaluates the full earn-and-redeem mechanic and decides it is worth joining

DO

Continue

3.0
Data Use Statement

Goal

  • Customer reads and accepts the plain-English data use statement before providing personal details

Description

  • Plain-language statement: "We track your purchases to give you rewards. We do not use your data to restrict your account."
  • Explicit separation from returns monitoring system
  • Marketing opt-in presented as a separate, optional choice — loyalty enrolment does not require it
  • Link to full terms (secondary — not the primary CTA)

DP

  • HMW design this screen so it feels like a trust-building moment rather than a legal obstacle — increasing rather than reducing enrolment confidence?

Scenario 2 In-Store Points Earning via App QR Scan

Scenario 2

In-Store Points Earning

A loyalty member makes a purchase at a Next store and wants to earn points on the transaction. She must be able to present her loyalty QR code at the till within 4 seconds of opening the app, see visible confirmation that points have been credited, and feel that the interaction is fast enough not to hold up the queue.

User Goal

Earn points on an in-store purchase quickly and confidently, with visible proof that the points have been credited to her balance

Business Goal

Capture in-store purchase data against a customer loyalty account — establishing cross-channel identity and driving habitual app engagement

Workflow: Variation 1 — Customer Self-Initiated App Scan Customer opens app, presents QR at till before payment

Workflow Steps

1.0 App Home Screen (Open Scan) 2.0 QR Code Screen (Customer presents to associate) {if scan fails} Er.1 Scan Error (Retry / Use Card) 3.0 Loyalty Confirmation Screen (Points earned shown) 4.0 Payment Screen (Complete payment) 5.1 Post-Purchase Balance Update
