Users

All distinct user roles in the system — real-world job titles, not persona names. Each role must have at least one object interaction in Table 3.1.

User RoleResponsibility
CustomerEarns and redeems loyalty points across online and in-store channels; manages account and preferences via web and app
Store AssociateIdentifies loyal customers at point of sale, applies in-store benefits, recognises loyalty tier status during service interactions
Store ManagerOversees in-store loyalty performance, monitors staff adherence to loyalty recognition protocols, reviews store-level redemption data
Marketing ManagerDesigns loyalty campaigns, sets point-earning rules, manages early access events, monitors programme engagement and ROI
CRM AnalystSegments loyalty members by tier and behaviour, builds targeted communications, analyses churn and retention patterns
Digital Product ManagerOwns the loyalty experience across app and web, prioritises feature delivery, manages cross-channel integration roadmap
Loyalty Programme AdministratorConfigures programme rules, manages tier thresholds, resolves point disputes, controls promotional mechanics
IT / Integration EngineerMaintains data sync between online and in-store systems, manages API integrations, resolves cross-channel data discrepancies*
Customer Service AgentResolves loyalty point queries, processes manual adjustments, handles missed points claims and redemption issues
Finance AnalystTracks loyalty liability on the balance sheet, monitors redemption rates, assesses programme cost and revenue impact
Next Finance ManagerManages integration of loyalty points with Next Finance credit accounts, assesses combined retention value*
Third-Party Brand PartnerAccesses loyalty data relevant to hosted brands (Reiss, FatFace, Joules) operating on the Next Total Platform*
2

Objects

All key entities in the system — always nouns. Never tools, verbs, or features. Every object must be touched by at least one role or object.

ObjectDescription
Loyalty AccountA customer's registered membership record in the loyalty programme, holding their points balance, tier status, and history
Points BalanceThe current accumulated total of loyalty points available to a customer for redemption
Points TransactionAn individual earn or burn event recorded against a customer's loyalty account, linked to a purchase or redemption
Loyalty TierA classification level (e.g. Member, Silver, Gold) that determines the benefits and recognition a customer receives
RewardA benefit made available to a customer upon reaching a threshold — including discounts, early sale access, or exclusive offers
RedemptionThe act of a customer converting loyalty points into a reward, discount, or benefit at checkout online or in-store
PurchaseA completed transaction by a customer online (next.co.uk / app) or in-store that triggers a points earn event
Customer ProfileThe unified record of a customer's personal data, purchase history, preferences, and loyalty status accessible across channels
Loyalty CampaignA time-limited promotional mechanic designed to boost engagement — e.g. double points events, seasonal bonuses, referral rewards
Early Access EventA sale or product launch made available exclusively to loyalty members before the general public, redeemable via app or web
In-Store Recognition AlertA real-time notification or display surfaced to a store associate when a loyalty customer is identified at the point of sale
App NotificationA push message sent to the customer's mobile device to communicate points earned, rewards available, or campaign updates
Loyalty DashboardA management view providing programme-level reporting on membership, points liability, redemption rates, and tier distribution
SegmentA grouped subset of loyalty members defined by behaviour, tier, spend, or recency — used to target communications and campaigns
CommunicationAn outbound message to loyalty members (email, push, SMS) containing personalised loyalty updates, offers, or campaign details
Programme RulesThe configured logic governing how points are earned, how tiers are assigned, and how rewards are unlocked and redeemed
Point DisputeA customer-raised claim that points were not correctly awarded or redeemed, requiring investigation and potential manual adjustment
Manual AdjustmentA corrective change to a customer's points balance made by a customer service agent or programme administrator
Integration LogA technical record of data sync events between online and in-store systems, used to diagnose cross-channel discrepancies*
Next Finance AccountA customer's credit account with Next Finance, linked to their loyalty profile to enable combined retention and spend tracking
Click & Collect OrderAn online order placed for in-store collection, representing a cross-channel touchpoint where loyalty earning and recognition can occur
ReturnA product returned by a customer in-store or online, triggering a reversal or adjustment of points previously earned on that purchase
ReferralA loyalty mechanic where an existing member introduces a new customer, triggering bonus points for one or both parties*
Loyalty ReportA periodic financial and operational report on loyalty programme performance, costs, redemption liability, and revenue attribution
EnrolmentThe event of a new customer registering for the loyalty programme, via app, website, or in-store point of sale
